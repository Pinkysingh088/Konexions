Ramendra Sudan

Co-founder
Ramender Sudan is a first generation entrepreneur having a unique combination of starting out with the Indian Government in Ministry of Finance & then along with his brother –in –law, gradually shifting to set up businesses as an entrepreneur and co-founder. Mr Ramender started his entrepreneurial journey by Co-founding Jumbo Logistics. A hard core operations specialist, Ramender demonstrated his Commercial and Operational abilities by building – up a sustainable business in just a couple of years’ time, catering to various corporate clients across the NCR. In the year 2007 Ramender Co-founded Konexions Back Office Services Pvt. Ltd., a start-up venture in business process outsourcing. Starting with modest beginnings Konexions is today a leader in BPO Services in India, across industries, serving leading enterprises in the banking, financial services, telecom, insurance & retail sectors in northern India. Ramender’s focused approach to enhance operational and delivery standards, implementation of Best Practices and technology has enabled Konexions to attain a leading position in the field of Outsourcing in India.

Mr.Rajeeev Dhawan

Mr. Rajeev Dhawan is associated with Konexions as Director - Consultant. He is a seasoned leader with over 35 years of rich and diverse experience with top consumer facing companies across Telecom Sector and Office Automation, viz. Bharti Airtel Limited, Tata Teleservices, HCL & Xerox India Limited (Erstwhile Modi Xerox Ltd.). His experience spans across Service Operations, Planning, Quality and Training. Prior to be associated with Konexions , Mr Rajeev was with Bharti Airtel Limited for last 17 Yrs. and signed-off as National Head - Collection & Retention,  He possesses vast working experience in Customer Service across businesses (Enterprise, Retail) & Geographies.   Mr. Rajeev commenced his career in 1989 as a Customer Engineer at Modi Xerox Ltd. and was later promoted to the position of Head of Service Planning at the national level. He then joined HCL in 2003 and has worked across customer service function at National level. He also served in Customer service function at Tata Teleservices Ltd. On the education front, Mr. Rajeev has done his schooling from Dehradun and is an Electrical Engineer, is also a Certified Lead Auditor.
