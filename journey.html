Our Journey

Our Journey Through the Years

2007

Nitin Chopra,Chopra a College Drop Out sets up a vision to enter into the BPM Industry - He always had the knack of creating something where he could help the youth of the country to get jobs! Nitin along with the co-founder Shivani started Konexions from the Living room of their house. As the business grew they expanded to the basement of the house and since then there was no looking back.

2010

Konexions Acquires 5 Logos and becomes a 500 people strong company.

2015

Racing forward,By now Konexions was serving 10 Global Giants and with footprints.

2019

Konexions Acquires Tech Soft Corporation and enters into IT Space.

2021

Konexions sets up ultra-premium centers in Gurgaon and Bangalore, redefining workspace culture and operational excellence.

2024

Konexions emerges as the first privately held company in India to set up an office in Dubai, marking its evolution into a global powerhouse.
